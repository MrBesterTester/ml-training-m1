### LoRA Fine-Tuning — Successful Run (REQ-020)

*Mar 12, 10:11 PM – 10:58 PM*

First attempt (REQ-019) crashed with a Metal OOM error. After restarting the machine and closing all apps, the retry succeeded:

- **600 iterations** completed in ~47 minutes
- Val loss: **2.809 → 2.037** (27.5% reduction)
- Train loss: **2.724 → 1.925** (29.3% reduction)
- Peak memory: **3.621 GB** (22.6% of 16 GB — the OOM was from competing apps, not the training itself)
- Throughput: 132–148 tokens/sec, 364,420 total tokens
- 3.473M trainable params (0.108% of 3.2B) — LoRA rank 8, 8 layers, Adam, lr 1e-5
- No overfitting: train/val gap only 0.112
- Diminishing returns: 85% of improvement in first 200 iters
- Output: `adapters/hw-diagnostics/adapters.safetensors` (13.9 MB) + 6 checkpoints + `results/training_log.txt`
